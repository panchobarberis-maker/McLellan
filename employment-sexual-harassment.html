Experienced Sexual Harassment Attorneys

Sexual harassment is a form of illegal sex discrimination under California's FEHA and federal Title VII. It includes unwelcome sexual advances, hostile work environment conduct, and quid pro quo harassment by supervisors, coworkers, or third parties. No employee should have to tolerate it.

McLellan Law Group represents employees in sexual harassment claims throughout Saratoga, San Jose, Cupertino, Los Gatos, and across Silicon Valley and Santa Clara County. We work on contingency — you pay nothing unless we win.
