Employment Litigation Defense for California Employers

California employers face some of the highest litigation exposure in the country. McLellan Law Group represents businesses of all sizes across Silicon Valley, Santa Clara County, and the Bay Area in wrongful termination claims, PAGA actions, wage and hour disputes, retaliation, and discrimination lawsuits.

Our goal is to resolve your matter as swiftly as possible while protecting your company's financial position and reputation. After resolution, we help reduce future exposure by reviewing HR policies, updating employee handbooks, and drafting compliant employment contracts and severance agreements.

PAGA Defense for California Employers

If your business has received a PAGA notice, time is critical. McLellan Law Group defends California employers against PAGA claims throughout Silicon Valley, Santa Clara County, and the Bay Area — advising on notice responses, asserting available defenses, and pursuing early resolution strategies to minimize per-violation penalties.
